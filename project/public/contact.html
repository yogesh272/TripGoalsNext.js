Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a package?

You can book a package by calling us at +91 77098 23098, messaging us on WhatsApp, or filling out the contact form. Our team will guide you through the entire booking process.

What is included in the package prices?

Our packages typically include accommodation, transportation, meals, guided tours, and entry tickets to attractions. Specific inclusions vary by package and will be clearly mentioned.

Can I customize my itinerary?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating customized itineraries based on your preferences, budget, and travel dates. Contact us to discuss your requirements.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation charges vary depending on the package and timing of cancellation. Generally, cancellations made 30 days in advance incur minimal charges. Please contact us for specific details.
